Adoption Agreement:       
On the day of adoption, we will ask you to sign an adoption agreement.  There will be an adoption fee of US$399 (minus a $50 rebate upon completion of your initial (FREE) training/obedience session with our trainer within the first 2 months of adoption).  All dogs are spayed & neutered, microchip, up to date with vaccinations & rabies shot, de-wormed and given heartworm preventative and flea medication every month.  We will provide the dog’s medical record for your reference. 
 
Once you adopt a dog from Adopt a Doggie, K9 Cooperhaus will offer one hour free group training consultation (www.cooperhaus.com).  Brent Cooper is the most effective trainer who we work with closely.  Please make an appointment with Brent Cooper for the training course after the dog settles in your home. You will receive a $50 rebate when you attend a training session with our trainer within the first two months of adoption.

Thank you for your considerations and giving a rescued dog a loving home.
