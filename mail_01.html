Hi Name,

This is Mary, the founder of Adopt a Doggie, rescuer and foster mom of many deserving dogs.  Thank you for your application to adopt Dog’s Name.  Please make sure that you have included your home photos with your application as we pre-approve all homes before adoption.  If you have not, please email a few photos of your home, yard, family, pets, and front of the house photos to adoptions@adoptadoggie.org for us to process your application. 

We are an international rescue group dedicated to help any dogs in need of a good home.  Some of our doggies are available for meet and greet while others doggies are being fostered abroad.  Dog’s Name is still being fostered abroad.  I know it is hard for you to commit before meeting the dog, but we have many loving families who understand the horrible situations in Asia.  All dogs are placed in experienced foster homes and we provide temperament tests before posting any dogs for adoption.  The only reason why we help with these poor dogs is because in Asia many people live in tiny apartments, work over 12 hours a day, and dogs are not treated as family members.  These dogs will never have a chance to survive and being in a home.

Every month we have 3-4 flights coming from Taiwan to San Francisco.  Our next flight is arriving on January 1, Thursday.  All the families that adopt our doggies abroad will pick up the dog at the SFO International airport.  Our volunteers will be at the airport to assist you and your family.

We do have a 30 days adjustment period.  In any case the dog is not a good fit, we have a boarding facility where you can return the dog.  We have never had to send a dog back to Taiwan.  We will re-home the dog locally.  However, this seldom happen as we are very experienced with dogs and all dogs are placed in good foster homes after being rescued.

Adoption Agreement:       
Once you are ready to go forward with the adoption, please go to the dog’s profile and on the right hand side “click” on Agreement.  Please email the signed agreement to adoptions@adoptadoggie.org.  There will be an adoption fee of US$399 (minus a $50 rebate upon completion of your initial (FREE) training/obedience session with our trainer within the first 2 months of adoption).  All dogs are spayed & neutered, microchip, up to date with vaccinations & rabies shot, de-wormed and given heartworm preventative and flea medication every month.  We will provide the dog’s medical record for your reference. 
 
Once you adopt a dog from Adopt a Doggie, K9 Cooperhaus will offer one hour free group training consultation (www.cooperhaus.com).  Brent Cooper is the most effective trainer who we work with closely.  Please make an appointment with Brent Cooper for the training course after the dog settles in your home. You will receive a $50 rebate when you attend a training session with our trainer within the first two months of adoption.

Thank you for your considerations and giving a rescued dog a loving home.
